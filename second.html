online advertising

An online advertising app is a digital platform that enables businesses to create, manage, and optimize ads across various online channels, such as social media, search engines, and websites. With features like audience targeting, budget control, and performance analytics, these apps help businesses reach the right customers and maximize their ad spend. Through automation and real-time data, an online advertising app can improve ad relevance and engagement, leading to higher conversion rates and better brand visibility. This technology is essential for companies looking to build their presence in a competitive digital landscape.

Technologies Used:HTML,CSS,JavaScript,php
